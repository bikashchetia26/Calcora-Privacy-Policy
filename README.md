# Privacy Policy for Calcora (All-in-one Calculator).

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to All-in-One Calculator. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data and what information is collected, used, or shared when you use the app.

1. Overview
All-in-One Calculator is designed to provide various calculators and tools for daily use, including health, finance, unit conversion, and engineering calculations. The app does not require user registration and we do not transmit calculation data to any server.
We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users.

2. Information We Do Not Collect
We do not collect, store, or share:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos or files
- Personal health records
- Financial information
All calculations are performed locally on your device.

3. Information Collected Automatically
Some non-personal information may be collected automatically for app functionality and improvement through third-party services.
This includes:
- Device type and model
- Android version
- App usage statistics
- Crash reports
- Anonymous performance data
This information is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you.

4. Use of Firebase Services
The app uses Google Firebase services for:
- Remote configuration of app features
- Improving app performance
- Crash analytics
- Controlling app updates and features remotely
Firebase may collect anonymous device and usage data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

5. Advertisements (AdMob)
The app displays limited advertisements using Google AdMob to support development and maintenance.
AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers
- Interaction data with ads
- Approximate location (for relevant ads)
This data is managed by Google and is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.
Users may see both test and real ads depending on app configuration.

6. Data Storage
The app may store the following locally on your device only:
- Favorite calculators
- App preferences and settings
This data never leaves your device.

7. Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Since no personal data is stored or transmitted, the risk is minimal.

8. Internet Usage
Internet is used only for:
- Loading advertisements
- Fetching remote app configurations
- Checking for app updates
- Currency Converter
The app works without internet for all calculator features.

9. Children’s Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. The app is safe for general audiences.

10. Third-Party Services Used
The app uses the following third-party services:
- Google Firebase
- Google AdMob
- Google Play Services
Each service follows its own privacy practices.

11. User Control
Users can:
- Use the app without providing any personal information
- Clear app data anytime from device settings
- Uninstall the app to remove all stored data

12. Permissions
The app does not request unnecessary permissions. Any permission requested is only used to provide core functionality.

13. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app and on the Play Store listing.
